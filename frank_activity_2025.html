Ongoing & Anytime tasks 2025

Ongoing Tasks:

There are normally a few slow weeks later in season when the garden of plants doesn't need much help. This is a great opportunity for a very important Activity: Stop -- Observe the natural life -- Just be. Feel the vibes of plants and bees to know you.

* Watering. all sites,
For Gburger & Electro-Pulse, get persons who live near those sites. Gburger needs water more often due to shallow soil depth.

*Pickup Litter (all sites)
* Stake up fallen plants
* Fallen Signs, Missing Signs – Drive them up or Replace

*Plant Sales
When we have extra plants in pots, and no new garden spaces, have plant sales at corner of Bank. Offer Potted plants for sale at Herbwalk and other Events.
When a date is set, a number of volunteers are needed to set-up and staff table.

Anytime Tasks:

* Plant Inventory of all plants in all sites. Species and location. Would be great if this can be charted in a graphic format. Otherwise just make a list.

* Research our Plant List for Butterfly "Host Plants" & note which butterflies use which plants

* Talk to homeowners/ property owners on Frank Street between Bank St. & Rideau Canal, encourage planting flowers, offer help if we are prepared to help. We may have potted plants to offer them.
