Community Associations are the logical hub where local people will seek links for gardening info & support.
Each CA has its own Committee for gardens. We hope one or more individuals from each of the following CA's will participate in this Central Ottawa info-sharing network:

  • Dalhousie CA Committee of the Public Realm
  • Centretown CA Trees Greenspace Committee
  • Lowertown CA Health & Wellness Committee
  • Old Ottawa East CA Parks & Greenspace Committee
  • Glebe CA Environment Committee
  • Glebe Annex @Glebeannex

Those individuals within each CA can share local news with this wider COGWG network. And report any relevant info to their local CA. No formality is required on the part of the CA.

All gardeners are encouraged to join your local Community Association

Have more fun by working together!
