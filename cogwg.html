Central Ottawa Garden Working Group

Let's talk Gardening! Whether you're an experienced gardener, a new gardener, or want to become a gardener!

We keep in touch by e-mails & meet in person. Please join -- send e-mail -- cmurray@proton.me
Want to start a chat group or social media page, please do! Does anyone know how to start a "Feed" on Bluesky?

1. Sharing experiences, extra plants etc.
2. Info & advice to start a garden -- seeds & tips for random planting in vacant spots -- asking property owners for permission
3. Compile a list of all plantings on properties managed by the City;
submit list to Coucilor's office & City departments annually, asking them not to destroy the plantings.

Community Associations are the logical hub. I've proposed this Working Group be adopted by Centretown CA's Trees Greenspace Committee, Dalhousie CA's Committee of the Public Realm, and Lowertown CA's Health & Wellness Committee.

Adopting this Working Group does not involve obligations for the CA's. Would be good if each CA has a representative in this Group.

All gardeners are encouraged to become members of your local CA.

Have more fun by working together!
